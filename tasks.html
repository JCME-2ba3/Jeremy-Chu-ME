41055 Assessment Tasks

As part of the UTS subject 41055 Professional Experience Review, I am required to complete several artefacts which demonstrate my professional ability for the engineering workforce. Links to these artefacts can be found below or in the dropdown menu, a brief description of each is given here.

Assignment 1 consists of the following artefacts:

  • 1a - Draft Portfolio V1: Curriculum Vitae

  • 1b - Peer review of other students draft portfolio and reflective question artefacts
  • 1c - Summary of received feedback to implement
